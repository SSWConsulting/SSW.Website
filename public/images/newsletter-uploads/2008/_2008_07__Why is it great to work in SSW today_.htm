Hi, my name is John Liu. I used to work at SSW for several years - right out of university. In 2005 I decided to explore the big world and see what other firms are like out there. It has been a very interesting journey through a few other consulting companies, but eventually the journey has led me back to SSW. One of the things I’ve really missed on this trip is the exposure to technology and the access to technical expertise here at SSW. Yes, there are many interesting places and projects out there, but if you want to really live on the cutting edge in Sydney, very few places will come close to what SSW has to offer.

Oh, here’s a shout out to a few of the ex-SSW employees out there, working at SSW is as fun as ever, don’t be a stranger and catch me up for a coffee!

I really did miss the SSW Rules :)

John
