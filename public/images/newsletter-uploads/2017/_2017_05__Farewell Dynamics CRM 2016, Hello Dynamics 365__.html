Farewell Dynamics CRM 2016, Hello Dynamics 365

In December 2016, Microsoft released a new update to Dynamics CRM on-premises and it’s an update that might raise some eyebrows. The most obvious change is the name: 'Dynamics CRM' is no more – say hello to 'Dynamics 365'.

Essentially that is just a name change but why will that lead to many companies, that need to choose between SalesForce or Dynamics, deciding that best solution for their company is Microsoft's Dynamics? Well today if they need to integrate with other systems, both solutions require quite some cobbling together. Companies generally care what the best solution is for them over the next 10 years. So let me explain what's sweet about what is coming from Microsoft.
