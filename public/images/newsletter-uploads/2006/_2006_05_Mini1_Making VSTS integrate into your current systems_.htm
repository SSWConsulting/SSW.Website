SSW Mini Update: Complimentary Event - Making VSTS integrate into your current systems -  Australian Tour!

READY to ROCK the LAUNCH - Australia Wide in MAY!

Visual Studio Team System (VSTS) is the new technology from Microsoft that integrates the whole development cycle from initial design, testing and development as well as bug tracking and project management tools. In this session Adam Cogan, SSW Chief Architect and Australian Microsoft Regional Director, will take you through everything you need to know to optimise your software development cycle using VSTS:

  • How a bug tracking system works
  • Customizing Team Foundation Server (TFS) to work in your organization
  • Updating your bug tracking system to work with TFS
  • How a task lists and work items work

Adam will also compare VSTS with its alternatives.

So come and join in the fun and Get Ready to ROCK the Launch!
