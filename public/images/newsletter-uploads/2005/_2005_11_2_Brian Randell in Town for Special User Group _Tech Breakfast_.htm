Sydney .NET User Group 30 November 2005

Up & Running with Team System

Creating good software solutions is hard. From design to code cutting, testing, deployment and project management, a software project's success requires effective team communication and tools that help, not hinder your process.

Brian Randell will discuss how VS Team System can help you increase the predictability of success of your software projects and you will quickly learn what Team System is all about.

By the end of this User Group you will have a greater knowledge of Team System and be able to get your own projects up and running with Team System.
