Featured Product: SharePoint 2010

With the 2011 SharePoint conference only a few weeks away, we decided to make SharePoint 2010 our feature product this month

​Microsoft SharePoint 2010 makes it easier for people to work together. Using SharePoint 2010, your people can set-up Web sites to share information with others, manage documents from start to finish, and publish reports to help everyone make better decisions.

Organizations are extremely good at accumulating data. Mostly in the form of documents and records, this data is often haphazardly located across the organization, and poorly integrated into document processes.
SharePoint 2010 aims to improve the situation, by storing all documents & records in a centralized system. They can store every version of a document, allow you to enforce information management policies, and improve efficiency by automating common business processes.
