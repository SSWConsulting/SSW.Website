Adam's 2024 Year in Review

It’s time to discuss 2024’s highs and lows. Definitely, the high was the successful acquisition of TinaCMS – it is a product we’ve used and loved for a while and it was great fun to work with their team and get to travel to plenty of conferences to promote it. I have mixed feelings about my daughters flying the nest, and happy I dodged a melanoma… hang on a sec… 2 melanomas!

Every year after Christmas I sit down with my girls to do a Retro of the previous year, set some goals for the upcoming year and hear what is important to people close to you.

I’d love to share some of the highlights of our year with you.
