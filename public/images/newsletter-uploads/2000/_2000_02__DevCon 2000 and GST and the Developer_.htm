1. THE ACCESS/ASP/SQL SERVER USER GROUP
===================================================

The MARCH "Microsoft Access/ASP/SQL Server User Group, Sydney" is scheduled for Wednesday 15th February 2000, 5:45 pm

1) We are having a discussion on what the GST means to us and our clients (as developers):

The ABN: Who’s eligible and what does it mean?
The GST: Its impact on your business and your database.
PAG vs PAYE:The new and the old. How will these changes affect you?
BAS: The Business Activity Statement is to be lodged every three months and will have a running effect on your business.

2) What are those DTS Tools in SQL Server?
Also Eris McEncroe will give a presentation on organising data for using Business Intelligence applications ( like ad hoc query, reporting and OLAP systems). Eris will cover the advantages of Star schema over Normalised database schema for this type of application. The presentation will include a demo showing how Data Transformation Services (DTS) can be used to restructure data into the required format. DTS, which ships with SQL Server 7, enables data to be extracted from just about any source, scrubbed,transformed and then pumped to a desired destination. Neither sources or targets needs to be a SQL server database. The whole process can be automated and scheduled to run as required.

Attending the user group is free and is an excellent opportunity to share ideas and pick up hot development tips.
