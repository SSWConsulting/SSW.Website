The African Roadshow October 2004 - Nairobi, Dar es Salaam, and Johannesburg

G'day Developers!


We are very excited to present this premier event to the .NET community in Africa.
Microsoft Regional Director in Australia Adam Cogan will be flying in to present some of the hottest topics currently being discussed at other core .NET conferences all over world. More information on The African Roadshow October 2004 .

Adam is the Chief Architect at SSW and a Microsoft Regional Director for Australia. He has recently returned from Tech Ed Kuala Lumpur in Malaysia where the topics he presented had the audience on the edge of their seats! Adam can be found presenting on .NET in all corners of the globe. Some of his recent conferences include Tech Ed 2004 in San Diego, New Zealand and Australia. He is also the president of the Sydney .NET User Group, and is presenting at the upcoming tour of Australia on Microsoft Access: Upgrading and Migrating to SQL Server and .NET in November and December 2004 with other top Microsoft speakers. View more conferences and events where Adam has spoken.
