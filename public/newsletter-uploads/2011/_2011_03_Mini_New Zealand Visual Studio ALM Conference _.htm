New Zealand Visual Studio ALM Conference

7 days to go!

Date: 6th & 7th April 2011
Where: Amora Hotel, Wellington (Formerly the Duxton)
Register: https://nzalm2011.eventbrite.com/ Cost: $395 NZD

The New Zealand Visual Studio ALM Conference is the premier event for developers in New Zealand. The inaugural event takes place in Wellington in less than two weeks and we wanted to make sure you don't miss it.

If you're involved in any way with software development, this is a conference that can't be missed. There's sessions with something for Project Managers, Business Analysts, Architects, Developers, Testers, Designers and much more.

At just NZD$395 per attendee, the conference offers exceptional value especially when you consider what you're getting.

  • Leading local and international presenters from as far away as the United States
  • 2 great keynote events and 20 breakout sessions over two days
  • 2 optional pre-conference training courses
  • 4 optional post-conference deep dive workshops
  • Lunchtime vendor sessions
  • A free 5-user license or Urban Turtle, the leading Scrum tool for TFS (valued at NZD$600)
  • A discount code for 40% off the "Professional TFS 2010" Book or eBook from Wrox Press
  • A chance to win one of 10 copies of TeamCompanion. (valued at NZD$200)

ACT NOW - More information online at https://www.nzalm.co.nz

Featured post-conference workshop

The SQL Server Step Up workshop is a one day deep dive training session. The Step Up has been specifically designed as a quick and concise way of finding out what is new in SQL Server 2008 R2 and the next release of SQL Server code-named 'Denali'. In this workshop you will have the opportunity to see how the latest releases of SQL Server will improve database operations, developer efficiency and enable self-service Business Intelligence solutions.
