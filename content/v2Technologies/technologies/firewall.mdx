### Firewall

Secure and reliable firewalls for peace of mind and protection from Virus' and
hackers. E.g. [Cisco ASA 5508-X](https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/support/security/asa-5508-x-firepower-services/model.html)
for servers and [Microsoft 365 Defender](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/security/defender/microsoft-365-defender?view=o365-worldwide)
for any device connected to the network.
