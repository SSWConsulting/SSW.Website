You can now have Active Directory, Exchange, SharePoint, Skype for Business,
OneDrive, and even classic Office apps like Word, Excel, etc. all hosted on
the Microsoft cloud under Microsoft 365 (was Office 365). Say goodbye to your
old server stack and focus more on your core business.
