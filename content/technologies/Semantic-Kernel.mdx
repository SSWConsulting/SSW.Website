Semantic Kernel is a cutting-edge tool that is central to an AI Copilot system. Designed to blend different AI services with conventional programming languages such as C# and Python, it allows for the development of complex AI-powered applications. Developers can combine programs with AI models to orchestrate plugins for ChatGPT, Microsoft 365 Copilot, or other models. It enables AI development to be flexible, with connectors and plugins as a the "brain" and functions to interact with the real world as the "body" of an app.
