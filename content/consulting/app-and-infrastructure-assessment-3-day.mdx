SSW’s App and Infrastructure Assessment and Scoping service provides you with a detailed architecture diagram and strategic roadmap, enabling you to make informed decisions about your legacy system's future.

Our assessment and scoping offering is designed to thoroughly evaluate your existing application and infrastructure or conceptualize a new cloud-native project from the ground up utilizing Azure for your cloud infrastructure.
