# The benefits of **Dynamics 365**

<VideoEmbed url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HWeskYHfwio" caption="Microsoft Dynamics CRM at SSW - Take it to the Next Level!" duration="2 mins" />

<VideoEmbed url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PV9w_rFsL1E" caption="Microsoft Dynamics 365 - A Developers Overview" duration="3 mins" />

<VideoEmbed url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=loBH0g6N0zM" caption="Microsoft Dynamics 365 - Case Study" duration="1 min" />

Managing your client relationships is easy with Dynamics 365. It combines
existing Microsoft products such in the Dynamics 365 suite (Sales, Customer Service, Business Central, Finance, Marketing, etc.) into one seamless solution. These components work together to allow your company
to track customers across the whole life cycle of their interaction with you.

You no longer need to worry about maintaining data across your CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems - all the information is seamlessly integrated.

Dynamics 365 allows you to pay only for the products you use - starting at $50/user per month.
