# The benefits of having a **Clean Architecture**

<VideoEmbed url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B9owh0BL7Y4" caption="Clean Architecture in 5 minutes with Jason Taylor and Piers Sinclair | NDC Melbourne 2022" duration="6 mins" />

Clean Architecture is a strategic framework that revolutionizes software development by ensuring your applications are not only robust but also flexible and future-proof. This architecture style is distinguished by its emphasis on the separation of concerns, creating a system that is easier to manage and evolve over time.
